Common Questions

Everything you're probably
wondering right now.

How long does an on-site assessment actually take?
Typically 90 minutes on your premises. We assess 30 dimensions across physical security, digital posture, and POPIA compliance. Minimal disruption — most businesses carry on normally while we work.
What does "48-hour delivery" actually mean?
Your written Aura Risk Score™ report lands in your inbox within 48 business hours of the site visit completing. If we miss that, the fee is partially refunded — we don't make promises we don't keep.
Do we need to prepare anything before the visit?
Nothing complex. We send a short pre-visit checklist after booking — just basic access to your premises and permission from any relevant staff. We handle everything else.
We already have a security company — why do we need this?
Security companies sell products and services. We sell independent advice. We assess whether what you already have is actually working, identify what they haven't told you, and give you a report your insurer can use. No conflict of interest.
What size businesses do you work with?
Primarily SMEs — businesses with 5 to 200 staff. Retail, professional services, healthcare, logistics, warehousing, and light industrial. If you have premises, clients, and data, you have risk we can quantify.
What happens after we get the report?
You own the report and the action plan. Many clients implement the quick wins themselves within a week. Others use us on a monthly retainer for ongoing monitoring. Either way, you leave with a clear, prioritised roadmap — not a vague list of problems.
